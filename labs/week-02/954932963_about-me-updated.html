About Me - Tanner Hogan

My name is Tanner Hogan and I am a junior graphic design major from New Jersey. Growing up, I was always fascinated by all types of art and design. I currently work as a digital design director for Jerk Magazine, a student publication here at Syracuse. Along with that, I began working as a graphic design intern for Syracuse Athletics this past June and plan to continue into the fall.

This past summer I declared myself as an IMT minor. IST 263 is one of two iSchool classes that I am taking this semester. These two classes are my first steps towards my minor, however, I took a few classes relating to computer science and web development in high school. I am excited to learn more about these topics and am looking forward to a great semester!
