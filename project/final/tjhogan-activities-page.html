Activites at the Jersey Shore

The Jersey Shore has a wide range of activites to keep travelers entertained on their vacation.

Mini Golf

Countless mini golf courses are scattered throughout the many beach towns in New Jersey. From Barnacle Bill's traditional mini golf course in Ortley Beach, to the pirate themed mini golf, Smuggler's Quay, in Seaside Heights, there is truly a place for everyone. This fun activity is a great way to bond with friends without breaking the bank. Average games cost about $10 per person.

Beach Concerts

Throughout the summer season, many beaches organize artists and bands to perform on the sandy shores to entertain summer crowds. Most beaches offer weekly shows in the evening to get vacationers on their feet and dancing. These family-friendly events are great to meet up with friends, eat some snacks, and drink some cocktails.

Boardwalks

The boardwalks on the coast of New Jersey are some of the most fun and entertaing in the world. With snacks like funnel cake, fried oreos, and classic Italian sausage, pepper, and onion sandwiches, visitors are sure to leave with a full stomach. The many rides, games, and arcades keep vacationers of all ages entertained for hours.
